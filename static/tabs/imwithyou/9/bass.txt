believe to be the toughest song on the album, I thought I would tab it out
for you UG'ers to learn. (Plus there's a bass solo too!)

intro so if there are some missing please let me know. Also let me know if
the fills are incorrect because they can be tough to hear in this song
anyways. If in doubt, play your own! :)
