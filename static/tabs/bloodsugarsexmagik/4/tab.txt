/or\   Legato slide   (slide to the next note without striking it)
b      Bend           (assume as quarter bend if it is by itself. Ex, 3b NOT 3b4)
r      Release bend   (release bend back to original position)
h      Hammer-on      (strike note with fretting hand without the pick attack)
p      Pull-off       (quickly pull fretting hand off of the note without pick attack)
~~~    Vibrato        (rapidly bend and release to give a tremolo effect)
()     Ghost Note     (note rung out into other notes)
tr~~   Trill          (hammer-on and pull-off rapidly between the 2 notes shown)
.      Staccato notes (make the note short and choppy)
+      Tapping        (use pick or index finger to hammer on note)
&gt;      Pick scrape    (scrape the pick down the strings towards the bridge)
&lt;      Pick scrape    (scrape pick down the strings towards headstock)
